Advanced Practice Nursing Clinicals

Genesis Healthcare Associates has established a history of supporting Advanced Practice Nurses by offering Clinical Nurses Practicums in a student-designed experience focusing on the role of the professional nurse in a variety of clinical practice specialties. These include: Internal medicine, pediatrics, family practice, and women’s health. The purpose being to gain knowledge, skills, and attitudes surrounding quality (and safe) patient care. There is a nominal fee for these services. Interested students can contact us at GenesisNPstudent@gmail.com for further information.
